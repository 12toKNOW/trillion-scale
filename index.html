Assumptions

A US $100 bill is 0.0043 inches thick. A year is 365.25 days. Interest is simple annual yield divided into seconds — no compounding, no taxes, no judgment. Counting assumes one dollar per second with no sleep, which is roughly how month-end close feels anyway.
